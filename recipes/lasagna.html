Lasagna

Description

Simple Lasagna recipe that your family will love!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Lasagna Noodles
  • 8 oz Tomato Sauce
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 2 cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta until fully cooked.
  2. Season ground beef with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook ground beef.
  4. Layer pasta in an empty baking sheet.
  5. Between each layer, spread tomato sauce, ground beef and cheese.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
