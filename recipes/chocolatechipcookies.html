Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies that are soft and chewy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 Cups Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Cup Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking soda, salt in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix butter sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix dry ingredients into wet ingrediets until combined. Add in Chocolate Chips.
  4. Use an ice cream scooper to form dough into balls.
  5. Place evenly on a cookie sheet and cook 8-10 minutes at 375 degrees.
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
