Baked Chicken

Description

Healthy and delicious oven baked chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Chicken Breast
  • 1 tbsp Avocado Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • Chopped Parsley for Garnish

Steps

  1. Coat raw chicken breast with avocado oil.
  2. In a bowl, mix salt, pepper, and smoked paprika. Coat chicken evenly.
  3. Place chicken in an glass oven dish. Cover with tin foil and cook for 20 minutes at 350 degrees.
  4. Let cool and enjoy.
